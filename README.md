# Mind Matters

**Mind Matters** is a mod for the game RimWorld that adds a more detailed psychology system to the game. It simulates the development of mental conditions based on a pawn's experiences, traits, and other factors, making the psychological aspect of survival even more important and immersive.

## Features

- **Experience System:** Pawns accumulate experiences based on their daily activities and interactions. Both positive and negative experiences are tracked. TODO - nothing positive is presently tracked.
- **Anxiety and Trauma Systems:** Pawns can develop anxiety or trauma based on their negative experiences. The likelihood of developing these conditions depends on the pawn's traits and the severity of the experiences.
- **Trait System:** Pawns can gain or lose traits based on their experiences. Traits also influence a pawn's susceptibility to anxiety and trauma.
- **Immunizing Traits:** Certain traits, like Psychopath and Desensitized, make a pawn immune to the effects of acquired experiences.
- **Scapegoat:** If your colony is large enough, a pawn may be picked (based on their traits) to have the role of scapegoat. If the scapegoat is happier than another pawn, the other pawn may blame them for problems in the colony and thus gain a mood boost. (I called someone out +6)

## Installation

1. Download the latest release from the [Releases](https://github.com/cemacmillan/MindMatters/releases) page.
2. Unzip the downloaded file into your RimWorld's `Mods` folder.
3. Activate the mod in the mod menu in-game.

## Compatibility

Mind Matters should be compatible with most other mods. If you encounter any issues, please report them on the [Issues](https://github.com/cemacmillan/MindMatters/issues) page.
